Spy x Family is a comedy about a master spy, Twilight, who must form a fake family as part of a mission to prevent a war. Unbeknownst to him, his adopted daughter Anya is a telepath, and his new wife, Yor, is a deadly assassin. To succeed in his mission, he must get Anya enrolled in an elite school to get close to his target, but the family's true identities and secrets must remain hidden.
