Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood is a fantasy-action series about two brothers, Edward and Alphonse Elric, who seek the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies after a failed alchemical ritual to resurrect their mother costs Edward his limbs and Alphonse his entire body. The story follows their journey, where Edward joins the military and receives the title of "Fullmetal Alchemist," while the brothers uncover a massive government conspiracy involving mysterious immortal beings called homunculi.

Author: Hiromu Arakawa

Original Title: Hagane no Renkinjutsushi: Fullmetal Alchemist

Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Dark Fantasy,Steampunk, Thriller, Horror, Comedy

Released Year: 2009
