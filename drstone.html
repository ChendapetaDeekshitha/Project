Dr. Stone is a manga and anime series about the genius boy Senku Ishigami, who aims to rebuild civilization with science after a mysterious light petrifies humanity for 3,700 years. Senku revives himself and his friends, encountering a strong rival, Tsukasa, who wants to create a new world order based on strength, leading to the "Stone Wars". The story follows Senku's "Kingdom of Science" as they introduce modern scientific advancements to the Stone World, face challenges, and ultimately embark on a global journey to find the source of the petrification.

Author: Riichiro Inagaki

Original Title: Dr. Stone

Genre: science Fiction, Adventure

Released Year: 2019
